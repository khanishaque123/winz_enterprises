Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays).

Important Note

Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on destination, weather conditions, and carrier delays. You will receive tracking information once your order ships.

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

International Delivery Times

  • Canada & Mexico: 5-10 business days
  • Europe: 7-14 business days
  • Asia & Australia: 10-21 business days
  • South America & Africa: 14-28 business days

Customs & Import Duties

International customers are responsible for any customs fees, import duties, or taxes that may be applied by their country. These charges are not included in the product price or shipping cost.

Order Processing & Tracking

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST are processed the same business day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by:

  1. Logging into your account
  2. Visiting the "Order History" section
  3. Clicking on your order number
  4. Using the tracking link provided

Changing Shipping Address

You can change your shipping address within 1 hour of placing your order by contacting customer service. After 1 hour, changes may not be possible as orders enter processing.

Shipping Restrictions & Limitations

Restricted Items

Some items cannot be shipped to certain locations due to legal restrictions or manufacturer limitations. These include:

  • Certain electronics to specific countries
  • Beauty products containing specific ingredients
  • Items with battery restrictions

Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver all orders on time, occasional delays may occur due to:

  • Weather conditions or natural disasters
  • Carrier service disruptions
  • Customs processing for international orders
  • Incorrect or incomplete address information

Need Help?

If you have questions about shipping or need assistance with your order, contact our customer service team at support@WInz Enterprises.com or call 1-800-WInz Enterprises.
