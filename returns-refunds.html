Returns & Refunds

Our hassle-free return policy and refund process

Return Policy Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a simple and straightforward return process.

1

30-Day Window

Return most items within 30 days of delivery

2

Original Condition

Items must be unused with original packaging

3

Tags Attached

All original tags and labels must be present

4

Proof of Purchase

Order confirmation or receipt required

Free Returns

We offer free return shipping for defective items or incorrect shipments. For other returns, a $7.99 return shipping fee will be deducted from your refund.

How to Return an Item

Step-by-Step Return Process

  1. Initiate Return: Log into your account and visit "Order History" or contact our customer service team.
  2. Print Label: We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label (for eligible returns).
  3. Pack Items: Place the item in its original packaging with all tags attached.
  4. Ship Return: Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location.
  5. Track Return: Use the provided tracking number to monitor your return.

Return Shipping Address

Important Note

We recommend using a trackable shipping service and retaining your shipping receipt until your refund is processed. We are not responsible for return packages lost in transit.

Refund Policy

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed automatically to your original payment method.

Refund Timeline

1

Return Received & Processed

3-5 business days after we receive your return

2

Refund Initiated

Within 24 hours of return approval

3

Refund Appears in Your Account

5-10 business days depending on your payment method

Refund Methods

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Store Credit: Instant processing
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days

Non-Returnable Items

Certain types of items cannot be returned for hygiene, safety, or legal reasons. These include:

Final Sale Items

  • Clearance or final sale merchandise
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Gift cards and downloadable software
  • Intimate apparel, swimwear, and hosiery (for hygiene reasons)
  • Opened cosmetics, fragrances, and skincare products
  • Food, beverages, and perishable goods
  • Health and safety items (face masks, etc.)

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We'll arrange a free return and send a replacement or issue a full refund.

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer service team is here to help! Contact us at support@WInz Enterprises.com or call 1-800-WInz Enterprises for assistance with returns, exchanges, or refunds.

Exchanges

Product Exchanges

We currently don't offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item for a different size, color, or product:

  1. Return the original item following our standard return process
  2. Place a new order for the desired item
  3. Once your return is processed, you'll receive a refund for the original purchase

Expedited Exchanges

For faster service, you can place a new order immediately and return the original item separately. This ensures you receive your desired item without waiting for the return process to complete.

Holiday Return Policy

Items purchased between November 1st and December 24th may be returned until January 31st of the following year. Regular return policy applies to all other purchases.
