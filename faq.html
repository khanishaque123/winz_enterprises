We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition with tags attached. Some items like clearance merchandise or personalized products may be final sale.

To initiate a return, visit our Returns Center or contact our customer service team. Return shipping is free for defective items or incorrect shipments.

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method:

  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days
